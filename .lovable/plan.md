

# Portfolio Website for Data Analytics & MEL Professional

## Overview
A clean, modern, single-page portfolio website with smooth scroll navigation. Navy blue and white color scheme with dark mode support. Executive-friendly design focused on credibility and scannability.

---

## Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Fixed top navigation with smooth scroll links
- Logo/name on the left
- Section links: What I Do, Projects, About, Skills
- Dark mode toggle button
- Contact Me button (highlighted)

### 2. Hero Section
- **Headline**: "Researcher, Statistician, Data Analytics & MEL Professional"
- **Subheadline**: Your value proposition
- **Credibility line**: 8+ years experience with tags for specializations
- **Stat highlights**: Three subtle cards showing 8+ Years, Business & Social Impact, Data-Driven Support
- **CTAs**: Primary "View My Projects" button, secondary "Download CV" and "Contact Me" links

### 3. What I Do Section
- Section title with subtle divider
- **Four clean cards** in a grid layout:
  - Data Analysis & Visualization
  - Machine Learning & Statistical Analysis
  - Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)
  - Data Systems & Quality Assurance
- Each card with an icon, title, and description

### 4. Selected Projects Section
- **Four detailed project cards** with expandable or stacked layout:
  - Sales & Operations Analytics (Alibert Products)
  - MEAL System Design (LevelUpRecyclers)
  - Taxi Fare Prediction ML (Google Capstone)
  - Sales Performance Dashboard
- Each project displays: title, organization, summary, problem, what you did, tools used, impact, and key insight
- Clean typography with clear visual hierarchy

### 5. About Me Section
- Professional summary text
- Clean, minimal presentation with subtle background differentiation

### 6. Skills Section
- **Four organized skill categories** in a grid:
  - Analytics & Tools (Python, R, Excel, Power BI, etc.)
  - Methods (EDA, Statistical modeling, ML, etc.)
  - MEL & Research (Indicators, Logframes, etc.)
  - Systems (Cloud systems, Data governance, etc.)
- Skills displayed as clean tags/badges

### 7. Footer CTA Section
- Strong closing message: "Interested in working together?"
- Two prominent buttons: Download CV & Contact Me (email link)
- Optional: Social/LinkedIn link

---

## Design Details
- **Colors**: Navy blue (#1e3a5f) accent on white, with dark mode support using navy-dark tones
- **Typography**: Clean, professional sans-serif with clear hierarchy
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace for executive readability
- **Mobile responsive**: All sections adapt gracefully to smaller screens
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in effects on scroll for polish

---

## Technical Notes
- CV download will use a placeholder link (you'll upload your actual CV file later)
- Contact button opens default email client with your address
- Dark mode preference saved to browser storage
